The Best Movies According to Kierstin

My Top 3 Favorite Movies of All Time

1. Everything, Everywhere, All At Once

This movie is incredibly good. I was initially skeptical because it is produced by A24, a studio I have personal issues with. However, it blew all of my expectations out of the water. I highly recommend this movie.

2. The Princess Bride

The Princess Bride is a timeless classic that the whole family can enjoy. Witty, humorous, and full of action and romance makes it a movie for everyone.

3. Spider-man: Into the Spiderverse/Across the Spiderverse

This one is a bit of a cheat but I consider them in the same caliber and a literally continuation of each other. They're brilliantly animated, styled, and there's incredible emotional depth to these that keeps one on the edge of their seat.
